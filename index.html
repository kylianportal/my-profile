Swimmer since my childhood

At the age of 5th I start to swim, I love to swim so i wanted to do it in competition.

I continue to love this sport and i start competition at 8. I learn from my brother's coach that i can do som paralympic competition with my eyes disibilities. So i started to do French championship, I was starting to get better.

So after many competitions I was selected in the French youth team. I do my first international competition at Helsinki in Finland for the EUropean Para Youth Game. It was such a great experience. After that I was selected for my first European championship, and that was so nice, even that I can't do final.

The next year I was selected for the 2022 World Championship, and I do a 4th place in final of the 400 freestyle !

The next year I was also selected for the 2023 World Championship, and I do a bronze medal with my brother ! That was so cool, the first podium I'm doing in my international career was with my brother become Wolrd Champion. That is the best memory of my career for the time being.

What's the ocular albinism ?

Albinism is an inherited genetic condition that reduces the amount of melanin pigment formed in the skin, hair and/or eyes. Albinism occurs in all racial and ethnic groups throughout the world.

People with albinism have vision problems hat are not correctable with eyeglasses,and many have low vision. It’s the abnormal development of the retina and abnormal patterns of nerve connections between the eye and the brain that cause vision problems. The presence of these eye problems defines the diagnosis of albinism.
